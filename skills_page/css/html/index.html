What do I excel at?

Here are some strengths in my work.

Data Analysis

As a design anthropologist, proper data analysis in user research goes a long way in ensuring the desired outcomes based on the data at hand. Includes synthesis of interviews, brainstorming sessions, and other research scenarios.

Information Architecture

User confidence in parsing through information should be at the forefront of all design. For example restructuring forms or content on a page to follow LATCH principles more closely.

Interaction Design

Going through and iterating through different layouts, such as effectively using A/B analysis to test designs and accurately interpret data from these types of tests.
